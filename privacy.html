This Privacy Policy describes how Realtime Interpreter AI (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information:

  • Microphone Audio: When you start a live interpretation session, the App captures audio from your microphone or system audio input. This audio is streamed directly to OpenAI's Realtime API for processing and is not stored by the App or on our servers.
  • Transcripts: The recognized source text and translated text are stored locally on your device. You can view, export, and delete these records at any time.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase and subscription transactions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store your payment details.
  • API Keys: If you choose to use your own OpenAI API key, it is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information through the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing real-time speech recognition and translation services
  • Storing your interpretation history locally for later review
  • Managing your purchased minute credits and subscription status
  • Improving app stability and performance through anonymized crash reports (only if enabled at the system level)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

The App shares data with third parties only as necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • OpenAI: Microphone audio, generated transcripts, and the selected target language are sent to OpenAI's Realtime API to provide translation. OpenAI processes this data according to its own privacy policy and API data usage policies.
  • Apple: Purchase transactions are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy governs how your purchase information is handled.
  • Developer Credential Service: If you use App Credits, a request is sent to the developer's backend (https://caolonghua.com.cn/api/realtime-credential) to obtain a short-lived API credential. This request does not include audio or transcript data.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services within the App.

4. Data Retention & Security

Audio data is streamed in real-time and is not retained after the interpretation session ends. Transcripts are stored locally on your device and persist until you delete them. No transcript data is transmitted to our servers.

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit, including TLS encryption for all network communications. Local data on your device is protected by the security features of your operating system.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access & Portability: View all stored transcripts and export them as DOCX files at any time.
  • Deletion: Delete individual interpretation records or all data from within the App.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Stop using the App at any time. The AI data processing consent prompt appears before each new session.
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For users in the EU (GDPR), California (CCPA/CPRA), China (PIPL), and Brazil (LGPD), additional rights may apply as provided by applicable law. Since all user data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. International Data Transfers

When you use the live interpretation feature, audio data is transmitted to OpenAI's servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. By using this feature, you consent to the transfer of your audio data to these servers.

We ensure that any such transfers are conducted with appropriate safeguards, including encryption in transit, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy within the App and on this page. The Effective Date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.

Continued use of the App after changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: 10000@caolonghua.com

We will respond to your inquiry within a reasonable timeframe.

我们不通过本应用收集姓名、电子邮件地址、电话号码或其他个人身份信息。

2. 我们如何使用您的信息

收集的信息仅用于：

  • 提供实时语音识别和翻译服务
  • 在本地存储您的传译历史记录以供日后查看
  • 管理您购买的分钟额度和订阅状态
  • 通过匿名崩溃报告改善应用稳定性和性能（仅在系统级别启用时）

我们不会出于营销目的向第三方出售、出租或分享您的个人信息。

3. 数据共享与第三方服务

本应用仅在提供核心功能所需时与第三方共享数据：

  • OpenAI：麦克风音频、生成的转录文本和选择的目标语言会发送到 OpenAI Realtime API 以提供翻译。OpenAI 根据其自身的隐私政策和 API 数据使用政策处理此数据。
  • Apple：购买交易由 Apple 处理。Apple 的隐私政策规定了如何处理您的购买信息。
  • 开发者凭证服务：如果您使用 App 额度，会向开发者后端 （https://caolonghua.com.cn/api/realtime-credential）发送请求以获取短期 API 凭证。 此请求不包含音频或转录数据。

我们不在本应用中使用任何第三方分析、广告或跟踪服务。

4. 数据保留与安全

音频数据以实时方式流式传输，传译结束后不会保留。 转录文本存储在您的设备本地，在您删除之前一直存在。 转录数据不会传输到我们的服务器。

我们采取合理的技术措施保护传输中的数据，包括对所有网络通信使用 TLS 加密。 设备上的本地数据受操作系统安全功能的保护。

  • 删除：您可以在应用内删除单个传译记录或所有数据。
  • 撤回同意：您可以随时停止使用本应用。AI 数据处理同意提示会在每次新会话前出现。
  • 投诉：您有权向当地数据保护机构提出投诉。

对于欧盟 (GDPR)、加利福尼亚州 (CCPA/CPRA)、 中国 (PIPL)和巴西 (LGPD)的用户， 适用法律可能赋予额外权利。由于所有用户数据都存储在您的设备本地，您始终可以完全控制自己的数据。

如果您认为有儿童通过本应用提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们将采取措施删除此类信息。

使用实时传译功能时，音频数据会传输到 OpenAI 的服务器，这些服务器可能位于您居住国以外。 使用此功能即表示您同意将音频数据传输到这些服务器。
